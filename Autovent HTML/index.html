Rules and Regulations

Two members per team. Inter-department teams are allowed. Rs. 40 entry fee per team for any one event in Round - 1 or Rs. 60 entry fee per team for both events in Round - 1 to be paid in cash at Room no. B307 (3rd Floor) on successful registration and for the payment you will be issued a receipt or you can pay online by the payment gateway in the registration link. Participants must carry their college ID card along with the receipt on the day of the event. Students must also bring their own pens and water bottles, masks and sanitizers.
